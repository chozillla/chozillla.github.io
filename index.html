About

My interest in hearing aids began when my sister was diagnosed with hearing loss. This inspired me to study Bioengineering at the University of Washington. My bachelor's thesis supervisor had completed his master's at the Technical University of Denmark in acoustics, which led me to pursue my graduate studies there.

During my time at DTU, I worked as a student assistant at Eriksholm Research Centre. Around the same time, LLMs emerged and I became deeply involved in working with them. Now, as a Discovery Engineer in the Discovery Team at Demant, I incorporate GenAI into hearing aid products while also driving the adoption of GenAI tools across the organization at a wider level.
